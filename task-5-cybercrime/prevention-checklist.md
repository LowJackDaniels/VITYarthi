# Stay Safe Online – Checklist (For College Students in India)

- Use strong, unique passwords for each account and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.  
- Never share OTPs, passwords, or PINs with anyone—even if they claim to be from a bank or official authority.   
- Verify UPI payment requests carefully; do not approve “collect requests” unless you initiated the transaction.   
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or social media messages—especially those creating urgency or offering rewards.  
- Download apps only from trusted sources like the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.  
- Regularly update your phone, apps, and antivirus software to protect against security vulnerabilities.  
- Check privacy settings on social media and limit the visibility of personal information.  
- Use secure Wi-Fi networks; avoid accessing banking or sensitive accounts on public Wi-Fi.  
- Log out of accounts when using shared or public computers.  
- Be cautious of online job or internship offers that ask for payment or personal details upfront.  

## Reporting Cybercrime
If you encounter any cyber fraud or suspicious activity, report it immediately:  
- **National Cyber Crime Portal:** https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- **Helpline:** 1930
