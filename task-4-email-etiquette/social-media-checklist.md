Do's:

-Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts and comments\
-Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation\
-Use privacy settings to control who can view your content\
-Share content that adds value, such as educational or informative posts\
-Think before posting—consider how it may affect your reputation\


Don'ts:

-Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly\
-Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content\
-Do not engage in online arguments or negative discussions\
-Avoid oversharing details about your personal life\
-Do not trust or click on suspicious links or unknown sources\
